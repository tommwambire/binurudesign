About us

Welcome to Alamsikhi Dragon Fruit Farm, a growing hub for sustainable agriculture and community empowerment in the heart of Kilifi County, Kenya. We are passionate about building a resilient food system through goat rearing, dragon fruit farming, and innovative approaches to community food sustainability.

At Alamsikhi Dragon Fruit Farm, we believe that lasting change begins at the grassroots. That’s why we champion joint ventures and partnerships—working hand-in-hand with local farmers, organizations, and community members to create opportunities, share knowledge, and strengthen livelihoods.

Our mission is simple: to create a meaningful difference from scratch. Through sustainable farming practices, community-centered initiatives, and an open invitation to collaborate, we are shaping a future where food security, economic empowerment, and environmental stewardship go hand in hand.

Join us on this journey—together, we can grow a thriving and sustainable future for our community.
